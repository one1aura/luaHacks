What is Lua?

Lua is a powerful, efficient, lightweight, embeddable scripting language. It supports multiple programming paradigms, including procedural, object-oriented, and functional programming. Its simplicity and speed make it an ideal choice for game development and scripting, especially within environments like Roblox.

Lua in Roblox Exploits
